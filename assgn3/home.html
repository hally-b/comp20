Take your dog for a short walk around your neighborhood to familiarize it with its new surroundings. When you arrive home, don't let your pet get too overwhelmed. Keep it in a single room to start and slowly introduce it to new spaces. Let your dog make a home in its crate! Lastly, be sure to schedule an appointment with the vet as soon as you can.
