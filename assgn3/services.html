At Dog Days, we have a great selection of animals in need of homes! We will provide the proper vaccinations and paperwork necessary to adopt an animal, so that all you have to do is choose a new pet! Contrary to a common misconception about adopted animals (especially Dobermans or German Shepards), not all animals came to live in our shelter because of an unstable mental state.
